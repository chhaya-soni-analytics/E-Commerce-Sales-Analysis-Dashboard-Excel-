# 🛒 E-Commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Overview

This project presents an interactive **E-Commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard** designed to analyze sales performance, profit trends, and customer behavior. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of business performance across regions, categories, and time.

It helps in identifying key revenue drivers, high-performing products, and areas impacting profitability.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall sales, profit, and quantity
* Track year-over-year (YoY) growth
* Identify top-performing products and customers
* Evaluate category-wise performance
* Understand regional sales distribution

---

## 📊 Key Metrics

* 💰 **Total Sales:** $2.29M
* 📈 **Total Profit:** $286K
* 📦 **Total Quantity Sold:** 37,873
* 🧾 **Total Orders:** 9,994
* 📊 **Profit Margin:** 12.47%

## 📌 Key Insights

* Technology is the most profitable category
* Sales show consistent growth with seasonal spikes
* Top customers significantly impact revenue
* Profit margin is slightly declining despite sales growth
* Certain sub-categories drive the majority of revenue

---

## 📌 Conclusion

This dashboard highlights the importance of tracking both sales and profitability. While the business shows strong growth in sales and orders, the decline in profit margin indicates a need for better cost and pricing strategies.

The insights from this dashboard can help businesses optimize product mix, target high-value customers, and improve overall profitability.
